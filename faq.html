Frequently Asked Questions

Have any questions? See if our FAQ section answers any of your concerns.
How do I order?

Head to the Beaded Jewelry Collection page to view all of our available products.

If you see anything you like, just click the product name or image. This will take you to the product page. You may press the "Add to cart" button after choosing the correct size for your accessory.

Once you've added all of your chosen items, click the "View cart" button on the top right of the website to checkout your cart and proceed with your payment.

After your payment, you simply just have to wait until your ordered item arrives at your door!

What are your payment options?

Currently, we are using PayPal as our Payment Gateway.

PayPal supports a wide range of payment channels including credit cards like Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
